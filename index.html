About Us

Our Goal

Coding Club, METIS at IITGN aims at spreading knowledge related to computer programming to the students and also develop websites and applications that cater to the needs of the institute.

Our Process

The club arranges weekly workshops focusing on basic programming and designing languages like JAVA and HTML. To inculcate a coding culture in the institute, Hackathon nights are conducted.
